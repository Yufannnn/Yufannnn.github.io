Morning Duty Tool and Night Duty Tool

  • Year: 2023
  • Institution: Raffles Institution Boarding School
  • Tools: Morning Duty Tool and Night Duty Tool
  • Team Lead: Led a team of three developers

This project involved leading a team to develop web and desktop tools designed to streamline task management for student tutors at Raffles Institution Boarding School. The tools include features for managing daily roll calls, morning checks, and prep checks, helping to organize and simplify routine administrative tasks.
