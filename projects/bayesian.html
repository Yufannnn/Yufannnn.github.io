Bayesian Stroke Prediction (CS3263 - Foundation of AI)

  • Year: 2023
  • Team Lead: Led a team of three students
  • Award: Best Project Award in CS3263 under Professor Leong Tze Yun

This project involved leading a team to develop a Bayesian modeling approach for predicting stroke risk. The model was designed to accurately assess the likelihood of stroke occurrence based on various medical and demographic factors, providing valuable insights into preventive care.

The project was recognized with the Best Project Award in the CS3263 course at NUS, under the supervision of Professor Leong Tze Yun.
