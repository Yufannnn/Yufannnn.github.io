tutorpro (CS2103T - Software Engineering)

  • Year: 2023
  • Course: CS2103T - Software Engineering
  • Team Lead: Led a team of four developers

tutorpro is a desktop software solution developed for private tutors to simplify their administrative tasks and enhance workflow efficiency. The software was designed to manage schedules, track student progress, and automate common tasks, allowing tutors to focus more on teaching.
