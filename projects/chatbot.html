Interactive Chatbot: Mr. Bear (CS2103T - Software Engineering)

  • Year: 2023
  • Course: CS2103T - Software Engineering
  • Project: Developed an interactive chatbot, "Mr. Bear", to assist users in task management and event scheduling

"Mr. Bear" is an interactive, text-based chatbot designed to help users efficiently manage their tasks and schedule events. Developed as part of the CS2103T Software Engineering course, Mr. Bear exemplifies the integration of AI and user-centric design to enhance productivity through a conversational interface.

Mr. Bear is a convenient and flexible software that assists you in managing and tracking all your tasks. It's:

  • Text-based: Interact with Mr. Bear using simple text commands.
  • Easy to learn: Designed with a straightforward interface that minimizes the learning curve.
  • FAST SUPER FAST to use: Optimized for speed, allowing users to manage tasks quickly and efficiently.
  • FREE: Enjoy all the features of Mr. Bear without any cost!

The Mr. Bear Application was created during the CS2103T 2022/23 Semester 2 module. This project builds on the foundation of Project Duke, serving as an educational tool that aids new software developers in honing their skills. Through the gradual construction of Mr. Bear, students learn and apply various software engineering principles, making it an excellent introduction to the field.
